Choose to remember

Never like before we have had the opportunity to capture our experiences as we go.

We have devices (they are not really just "phones" anymore) where we can capture video, take photos and write notes at any given time.

The problem is these devices are also a distracting tool that alienates us from living the present moment.

Still, the ability to capture memories overweights the downsides. However, a tool to better organize these memories in a way the human brain does is still needed. It's not a very complex problem, but not trivial.
